Hello, I'm Trent Kim (김강석). I am a designer and artist interested in the ways through which storytelling can aid the processes of healing, finding belonging, and searching for meaning. Through different modes of storytelling, my work looks to challenge the systems which standardize and alienate us in order to create meaningful connections and more complex understandings of the world.
